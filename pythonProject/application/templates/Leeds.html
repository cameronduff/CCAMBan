Leeds

Whitehall

Sky Leeds operates from two sites. You can find the contact centre in Central Square. The Digital Hub is located at Sky Whitehall. Both locations have a restaurant and coffee bar as well as a chill-out area with pool tables

Getting to Leeds

The Whitehall office is a short walk to the station. When exiting the station head to Aire street and follow it along for eleven minutes as it becomes Whitehall Road. Just after crossing the footbridge the office is on your right.

Passes and Access

As soon as you can raise a request on Spark to get a pass. Your manager should approve this for you and this will enable you access to the office. If your pass hasn't been printed yet ask your manager to grant you visitor access and someone will collect you from reception.
