ABOUT THIS WEBSITE

Welcome to your go-to resource for all things related to your journey at Sky! We’ve designed this website with one goal in mind: to make your transition from bootcamp to your first rotation as seamless and successful as possible. Starting a new role is both exciting and challenging, and we know how important it is to have the right support and information at your fingertips. That’s why we’ve packed this site with a variety of tools and resources tailored specifically to your needs as you navigate your transition.

  • Choosing Your Rotation: One of the first major decisions you'll make is selecting your rotation. We understand how crucial this choice is in shaping your career path, so we’ve provided guides, insights, and testimonials to help you make an informed decision. Whether you're looking to dive into a specific technology, explore different teams, or align your work with your long-term career goals, you'll find plenty of information to guide you.
  • Bootcamp Knowledge: The knowledge and skills you’ve gained during your bootcamp are the foundation of your success at Sky. But we also know that it can be easy to feel overwhelmed or lose track of what you’ve learned as you move forward. That’s why we’ve included resources to help you retain, reinforce, and build upon your bootcamp learnings - we’ve got you covered.
  • Orienting Yourself to Sky’s Spaces: Sky is a dynamic, fast-paced environment with a lot to offer. Whether it’s navigating our physical spaces or understanding our organizational structure, this website will help you get acclimated quickly. We’ve included maps, information about Sky's different sites, and tips on how to make the most of your time here. Our goal is to help you feel at home at Sky from day one.
