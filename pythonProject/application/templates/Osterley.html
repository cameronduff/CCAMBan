Osterley


Sky Central

Sky Central is the first place you'll go for your induction. The Osterley campus has 13 subsidised restaurants and cafes throughout the campus. The site also shows the latest films in its own cinema, has a beauty salon, laundry service and car wash. There is a gym on campus which also offers different classes. The box in sky central broadcasts the news live and other shows sets like SoccerAM are located on campus.

Getting to Osterley



The Osterley offices have a free on-site car park. You need to request a permit and register your car on the ParkMe app. It is first come first served and has electric charging points. There are also unmonitored overflow car parks. Sky also promotes ride sharing as much as possible to minimise the environmental impact of travelling by car.

The nearest train station to Sky Studios is Syon Lane. The nearest train station to Sky Studios is Syon Lane. Come out of the station, turn left and use the crossing immediately to your right to cross the road. Follow the footpath under the underpass which will bring you out on the other side of Great West Road. At this point turn right and follow the footpath until you reach Harlequin Avenue. Turn left down Harlequin Avenue and continue down the path and you will find the reception.

The free sky shuttle bus service stops at a few of the nearby tube stations. The nearest tube stations are Osterley, Ealing South and Ealing Broadway. The shuttle service runs from Ealing High Street and South Ealing station to the campus. Another service runs from Gunnersbury to the campus and one runs from the Osterley station. They come every 20 minutes. You can track these on the SkyShuttleBus app.

Passes and Access

As soon as you can raise a request on Spark to get a pass. Your manager should approve this for you and this will enable you access to the office. If your pass hasn't been printed yet ask your manager to grant you visitor access and someone will add you to a list at reception for approved visitors.
