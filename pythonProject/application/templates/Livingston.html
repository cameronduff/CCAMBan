Livingston

Watermark

Sky Watermark is Livingston's central site. The Livingston campus has a contact centre, gym, beauty salon and restaurant. All of which are connected to Watermark by shuttle bus. Inside Watermark itself there is a coffee bar and Watermark is open Monday to Thursday and does not open on Fridays. If you wish to work from the office on Fridays you can grab a desk at the contact centre.

Getting to Watermark

Car

The Watermark office has a free on-site car park. You need to request a permit and register your car on the ParkMe app. It is first come first served and has electric charging points.

Train

Many people choose to take the train from Edinburgh or Glasgow to the office. The closest train station to the campus is Livingston North. The train from Edinburgh is the Edinburgh Waverly to Helensburgh Central service. Sky runs a free shuttle bus service from Livingston North to campus. The service comes every 20 minutes from the station bridge.

Bus

If you are in Livingston the free sky shuttle bus service doesn't just stop at the station. It comes every twenty minutes and stops at St John's Hospital (Alderstone Road), the Shopping Centre Bus Station (outside Stack & Still) and Sky 2 (Baird Road).

Passes and Access

As soon as you can raise a request on Spark to get a pass. Your manager should approve this for you and this will enable you access to the office. If your pass hasn't been printed yet ask your manager to grant you visitor access and someone will collect you from reception.
